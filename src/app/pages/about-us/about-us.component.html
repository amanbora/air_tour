About Us

Even if you’re not under time pressure, there’s enough to keep track of at your gate—carry-ons, kids, boarding passes, passports—that an airport app needs to be truly useful to warrant even a quick look.
The Airsy Airport App earn their spot on your home screen by providing essential and timely information when you need it. Our app may not be the hottest, coolest, or most fun, but it's an app you will actually use while barreling to and through the airport.
