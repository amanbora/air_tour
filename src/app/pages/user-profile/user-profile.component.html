AIRPORT SERVICES ACTIVITIES

PASSENGER SERVICES

Passenger attention and assistance at the airport:

  • Passenger check-in.   
  • Flight connection assistance (transfer desk).
  • Passenger embarkation.
  • Passenger assistance in the event of flight problems.
  • Special passenger assistance (minors, VIP).
  • Computerised check-in systems.
  • Information desks.
  • Excess luggage charges.
  • Flight statistics report.
  • Luggage search services (search and distribution).
  • Flight editing.
  • Document verification.
  • Overbooking resolution.

LOADING OPERATIONS AND SHEETS

Assistance during the entire change-over of the aircraft by a specialised agent:

  • Communication between the cabin and the ground.
  • Assistance during the engine start-up.
  • Meteo.
  • Crew assistance.
  • Ramp coordination (ramp agent).
  • Crew briefing
  • Ground-to-air communication (VHF).
  • Load planning.
  • Control of ULDs.
  • Service coordination and supervision.
  • Preparation of loading sheet.

RAMP


Provision of services required by aircraft during their change-overs at airports:

  • Loading and unloading of suitcases and freight.
  • Dragging and thrusting of aircraft.
  • Drinking and wastewater services.
  • Cleaning of passenger and crew cabins.
  • Auxiliary equipment, tyre, electrical and air conditioning groups.
  • Transportation of passengers and crew between aircraft and terminals.
  • Transportation of suitcases and freight.
  • Handling of ULDs.

GENERAL AVIATION

Full assistance service to private aeroplanes and their passengers:

  • Flight assistance.
  • Passenger services.
  • Catering requests, hotel reservations and transportation for passengers and crew.
  • Loading and unloading of suitcases.
  • Auxiliary flight teams.
  • Coordination of payments and airport taxes.
  • Cabin cleaning.
  • Flight plans and meteorological information.

FREIGHT

Safekeeping, handling and delivery of freight at the freight terminal:

  • Documentation management (Importation/Exportation).
  • Special load handling.
  • Storage service.
  • Mail handling.
  • Load acceptance and consolidation.
  • ULD loading and unloading (freight units).
  • Inventory control.
  • Loading and unloading of lorries.
  • Rapid delivery services.
  • Handling of hazardous goods.

AIRPORT TICKET SALES

Reservation and sale of tickets for customer airlines:

  • Ticket desk.
  • Charging of complementary services (excess luggage, special services, etc.).

Handling PMR

Attention and assistance services to passengers with reduced mobility:

  • Facilitate the embarkation and disembarkation of passengers with reduced mobility through qualified people and specific teams.

Aircraft de-icing service.

Check-in of passengers and luggage at:
